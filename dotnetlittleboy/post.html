What is Dynamics CRM?

CRM stands for customer relationship management. It's a category of integrated, data-driven software solutions that improve how you interact and do business with your customers. CRM systems help you manage and maintain customer relationships, track sales leads, marketing, and pipeline, and deliver actionable data.

Posted by dotNetLittleBoy on Feb 24, 2020

Why we need Dynamics CRM 365?

  • Customer relationship management (CRM) systems allow for easy information tracking and sharing to ensure that each interaction with a customer is meaningful, productive, and pleasant
  • Beyond sharing information in CRM, integrating Dynamics 365 with other systems in the organization allows an individual to get all the information they need from one location.
  • Providing access on mobile devices ensures that the information is entered into the CRM and a manager can see reporting or insight in real-time. The value of a CRM within an organization is endless.
  • Dynamics CRM supports on-premise, in-the-cloud and partner-hosted deployment options.
  • Dynamics CRM have automation feature which helps to automate your Sales process, Marketing process, Case management process etc.

Plugins

Plugin are the custom business logic which we write on .Net Framework and build assembly we register on CRM to modify or extent the out of box behavior of CRM.

Plugins has two events -
1. Pre Event On Pre Event, plugin has two operation-
a) Pre Validation
b) Pre Operation

2. Post Event On Post Event, plugin has one operation-
a) Post Operation
